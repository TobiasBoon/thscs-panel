Help
  1. How will I get my pizza?
    Once you order through the control panel, come up to the pizza stand and pay for your order. We'll let you know when the pizza is here. Claim your pizza with your ticket.
  2. Are written scores combined with the programming?
    Nope! Written scores are separate, to give the smaller teams a fighting chance. Written scores shall only be used for the purpose of breaking ties.
  3. How do I appeal?
    At the end of the contest, you will be able to go through your individual runs and you will be able to see your output compared to the judges output. If we made a mistake, then hit the appeal button!
  4. How will I receive a notification if there is a new clarification?
    Your web browser is connected to our server via. websockets, so anything that we send out, you get it immediately. You'll even get a handy alert!
  5. Why can I only see my team's written scores?
    If we gave out the all of the written scores, how much fun would that leave for the awards ceremony? If you don't see your own team members scores, that means it hasn't been entered in yet, or your team member didn't write his name legibly :(
  6. Will I be able to preview my source code before submitting?
    Yes! We have a handy feature that shows you what you're submitting before you press that judge button. This prevents any accidental submissions!
  7. I have a question that is not here or something isn't working.
    Submit a general clarification if the contest has started, or simply raise your hand if it hasn't.
